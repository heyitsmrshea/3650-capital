Privacy Policy

This page summarizes the privacy practices posted for 3650’s U.S. website.

Effective date
November 15, 2022

Contact 3650 with privacy questions

At a glance

  • Applies to 3650’s U.S. websites where the policy is posted.
  • Covers contact, inquiry, device, employment, and commercial-interest data.
  • Explains use for operations, communication, marketing, security, and compliance.
  • Describes choices around email, cookies, analytics, and tracking tools.

Scope

Applies to 3650 websites in the United States.

The policy applies to 3650 REIT Loan Origination LLC and to the websites where the privacy policy is posted. It is intended for users located in the United States and does not automatically extend to affiliates, parents, or subsidiaries.

Information collected

Contact, inquiry, device, employment, and commercial-interest data.

  • Names, mailing addresses, telephone numbers, and email addresses.
  • Information submitted through contact forms, inquiry forms, or mailing list signups.
  • Device, browser, IP address, and operating-system information.
  • Employment-related materials such as resumes, employment history, and references.
  • Commercial-interest data related to services a visitor has considered or requested.

How information is collected

Directly, automatically, from third parties, and by combining sources.

3650 may collect information directly from users, through browsing activity on the platform, from third-party providers such as analytics vendors, and by combining online and offline information or data from multiple devices.

How information is used

Operations, communications, marketing, security, and legal compliance.

  • Operating and improving the website and business.
  • Responding to questions, feedback, and requests.
  • Communicating about the relationship, the platform, and legal terms.
  • Sending company or business-related marketing communications.
  • Protecting the platform, the company, and users.

How information may be shared

Affiliates, service providers, business partners, successors, and legal authorities.

According to the public policy, information may be shared with affiliated entities, vendors performing services on 3650’s behalf, marketing or analytics partners, successors in a merger or sale, or when disclosure is required to comply with law or protect the company.

Choices and controls

Email, cookies, analytics, and browser-specific settings.

The policy includes opt-out choices for marketing emails and explains that users can manage cookies, flash cookies, browser settings, Google Analytics choices, and image-loading behavior in email clients. The site does not currently respond to browser “do not track” signals.

Security and storage

Reasonable safeguards, but no guarantee of complete security.

3650 states that it uses reasonable means to safeguard personal information but does not guarantee that use of the internet or the platform is completely secure. Information may be stored in or outside of the United States, and use of the platform is subject to U.S. law.

Children and contact

Adults only, with compliance contact information provided.

The policy says the platform is intended for adults and that 3650 does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Questions or requests may be directed to compliance@3650capital.com or mailed to 2977 McFarlane Road, Suite 300, Miami, Florida 33133.
