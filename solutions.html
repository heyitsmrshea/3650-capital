Target investment summaries

Actual ranges for structure, leverage, term, and loan size.

Target ranges for structure, leverage, term, and loan size.

RECS

Senior, mezzanine, and preferred equity.

  • Structure: Senior secured loans, mezzanine debt, and preferred equity
  • Leverage: Up to 85% loan to cost
  • Term: 1-5 years
  • Loan size: Minimum $25M senior / $10M subordinate
  • Pricing: SOFR + 550 and 11%+ subordinate pricing
  • Scope: United States, all asset classes

Stable cash flow lending

Commercial and multifamily fixed-rate programs.

  • Term: 5 and 10 years
  • Loan size: Minimum $10M commercial / $5M multifamily
  • DSCR: 1.25x commercial / 1.20x multifamily
  • Fee: No origination fee; buydown on a case-by-case basis
  • Markets: Primary, secondary, and tertiary U.S. MSAs
  • Property types: Industrial, retail, self-storage, hospitality, office, multifamily, MHC, 55+, student

Special situations

Debt and equity flexibility.

  • Strategy: Technical and complex workout situations
  • Structures: Equity, preferred equity, mezzanine, debt, note purchases
  • Target size: $20 million+
  • Term: 3-5 years
  • Focus: United States, cross-asset CRE opportunities
